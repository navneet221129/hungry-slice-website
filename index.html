The Tech Behind the Taste

How we re-engineered the classic local takeaway into a high-performance food delivery model.

1

Traditional Takeaway

Local pizzerias focus on storefronts, leaving delivery to third-party drivers. Slices arrive cold, cardboard soggy. We knew Hamilton deserved better.

2

Cloud Kitchen Ops
